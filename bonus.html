Saffron Yarkand Hare

This large furry hare is found in Xinjiang, China. It has the typical long ears with a granular brown-colored fur coating its body and survives by burrowing underground. According to the IUCN, Yarkand hares are considered threatened creatures due to habitat loss and continuous hunting.
